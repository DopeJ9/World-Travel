‚ú® Tour Route & Experience

  1. Entrance Hall: Diplodocus Skeleton
    Right at the entrance, you'll see the huge Diplodocus fossil, the largest preserved in Europe.
    Kids will look up and go "Wow!" instantly amazed.
  2. Dinosaur Exhibition Area
    See various dinosaur skulls, kids can take photos while imagining themselves in "Jurassic Park".
    No need to examine every fossil in detail, just focus on the highlights.
  3. Archaeopteryx Fossil
    This is one of the world's most famous fossils, perfect for telling kids the story of "dinosaur to bird evolution".
  4. Glass Jar Specimen Collection
    Over 270,000 jars of preserved specimens, strange and mysterious.
    Kids will find it fascinating, adults might find it a bit "creepy".
  5. Quick Bird Exhibition Area (if time permits)
    Claims to cover 90% of the world's birds, kids can learn about many new bird species.
