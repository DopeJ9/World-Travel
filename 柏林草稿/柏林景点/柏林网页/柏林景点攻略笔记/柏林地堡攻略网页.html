Route Planning Overview

Tour 1: Dunkle Welten (WWII/History)

Directly choose the most classic WWII bunker tour, starting from Gesundbrunnen metro station.

Upon entering the air-raid shelter, the damp, cold air and thick concrete walls hit you immediately. The oppressive space instantly transports you back to the 1940s.

The exhibition tells the story of Berlin under bombardment. The iron gates and tightly arranged benches under dim lighting give you a visceral sense of how citizens squeezed into narrow spaces to survive.

Quick Visit to Permanent Exhibition

Take a quick overview of the bunker museum's core exhibition areas.

Focus on the display panels and original photos related to the rise and fall of the Nazi regime, from Hitler's youth to the final Battle of Berlin in WWII - a clear historical thread will quickly connect.
