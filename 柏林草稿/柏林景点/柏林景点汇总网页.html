Berlin Explorer
Discover the history, culture, and architecture of Germany's capital with our curated selection of top attractions.

Must-Visit Attractions & Booking

01

Berliner Dom

Baroque Cathedral

Berlin's most magnificent Baroque-style cathedral on Museum Island. Enjoy the stunning dome and panoramic city views.

Book Tickets
02

Fernsehturm

TV Tower

Berlin's iconic landmark standing at 368m. The observation deck offers 360-degree panoramic views of the city.

Book Tickets
03

DDR Museum

Interactive History

Experience daily life in East Germany through interactive exhibits, including a Trabant simulator and apartment reconstruction.

Book Tickets
04

Neues Museum

Archaeology

Home to the bust of Queen Nefertiti and extensive collections of Ancient Egyptian and prehistoric artifacts.

Book Tickets
05

Museum für Naturkunde

Natural History

Features the world's tallest mounted dinosaur skeleton and over 30 million specimens of minerals and animals.

Book Tickets
06

Berlin Story Bunker

Historical Exhibition

Located in a WWII air-raid shelter, documenting 800 years of Berlin history with a focus on the Nazi era.

Book Tickets
