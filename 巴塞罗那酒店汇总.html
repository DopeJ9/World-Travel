W Barcelona

⭐ 4.8 Five-Star Hotel
€800/night
This five-star hotel by Barcelona's beach (Barceloneta waterfront) is famous for its sail-shaped building, designed by renowned architect Ricardo Bofill. Almost every room or suite offers sea views. The hotel features a rooftop bar, infinity pool, direct beach access, and modern fitness and spa facilities.
Featured Highlights
  • Sea View Design — All rooms oriented toward waterfront views, the ultimate "sea view" accommodation option
  • Vibrant Atmosphere — Hotel positioning is more youthful and fashionable, suitable for vacation and socializing
  • Unique Architecture — The building's exterior itself is a landmark, excellent for photography
Suitable For: Travelers wanting sea views, vacation atmosphere, nightlife or social needs; families or friend groups also well-suited.

Hotel Ohla Barcelona

⭐ 4.2 Boutique Hotel
€480/night
Located in the heart of Barcelona's Gothic Quarter, Ohla Barcelona is a luxury boutique hotel combining art and historical atmosphere. Its facade is decorated with works by artist Frederic Amat—"A Thousand Ceramic Eyes" gazing in all directions. The hotel's interior design is sleek and modern, with a rooftop pool and observation platform.
Featured Highlights
  • Artistic Atmosphere — Building and interior design feature artistic and creative elements
  • Historic District — Located in the old city, within walking distance to multiple cultural attractions
  • Boutique Scale — Fewer rooms provide more privacy and customized service
Suitable For: Those who appreciate art, design, have old town exploration needs, and value atmosphere and location.

⭐ 3.9 Boutique Hotel

Featured Highlights
