Time slots are mandatory for the Brunelleschi Pass, Giotto Pass, and Ghiberti Pass. Please note that the reserved time slot applies only to your first site entry; subsequent visits to the other sites included in the pass can be made freely within the pass validity period.

Important Notice: The Brunelleschi Pass, Giotto Pass, and Ghiberti Pass are valid for 3 consecutive days. Each site included in the pass can be visited once during this period.
