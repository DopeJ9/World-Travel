Florence Must-Visit Attractions Guide
1

Cathedral of Florence

Gothic Cathedral
The magnificent Duomo with its iconic red-tiled dome designed by Brunelleschi. A masterpiece of Renaissance architecture and the symbol of Florence, offering breathtaking views from the top.
Book Tickets
2

Piazzale Michelangelo

Scenic Viewpoint
The most famous viewpoint in Florence offering panoramic views of the city and the Arno River. Perfect for sunset photography with bronze replicas of Michelangelo's David and other sculptures.
Book Visit
3

Basilica di Santa Croce

Franciscan Basilica
Known as the "Temple of Italian Glories," this Gothic basilica houses the tombs of Michelangelo, Galileo, and Machiavelli. Famous for its stunning frescoes by Giotto and beautiful Pazzi Chapel designed by Brunelleschi.
Book Tickets
4

Galleria degli Uffizi

Art Gallery
One of the world's most famous art museums, housing an incomparable collection of Renaissance masterpieces. Features works by Botticelli, Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael. The Birth of Venus and Primavera are among its most celebrated treasures.
Book Tickets
5

Bargello Museum

Sculpture Museum
Italy's premier sculpture museum, housed in a stunning medieval palace. Home to masterpieces by Donatello, Michelangelo, and Cellini. Features the famous David by Donatello and an outstanding collection of Renaissance bronzes and decorative arts.
Book Tickets
