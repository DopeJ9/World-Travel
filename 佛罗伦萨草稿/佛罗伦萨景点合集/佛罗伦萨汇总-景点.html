Discover Florence

Explore the birthplace of the Renaissance through its magnificent cathedrals, world-class museums, and breathtaking viewpoints.

Must-Visit Attractions

Cathedral of Florence

The magnificent Duomo with its iconic red-tiled dome designed by Brunelleschi. A masterpiece of Renaissance architecture and the symbol of Florence, offering breathtaking views from the top.

Book Tickets

Piazzale Michelangelo

The most famous viewpoint in Florence offering panoramic views of the city and the Arno River. Perfect for sunset photography with bronze replicas of Michelangelo's David and other sculptures.

Book Visit

Basilica di Santa Croce

Known as the "Temple of Italian Glories," this Gothic basilica houses the tombs of Michelangelo, Galileo, and Machiavelli. Famous for its stunning frescoes by Giotto and beautiful Pazzi Chapel.

Book Tickets

Galleria degli Uffizi

One of the world's most famous art museums, housing an incomparable collection of Renaissance masterpieces. Features works by Botticelli, Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael.

Book Tickets

Bargello Museum

Italy's premier sculpture museum, housed in a stunning medieval palace. Home to masterpieces by Donatello, Michelangelo, and Cellini. Features the famous David by Donatello.

Book Tickets
