Santa Elisabetta - This Pasta Completely Redefined "The Taste of the Sea" for Me

Santa Elisabetta

Fine Dining Experience in Florence

Florence | This pasta completely redefined "the taste of the sea" for me at Santa Elisabetta, a remarkable culinary experience that elevated my understanding of Italian seafood cuisine.

Prime Location
The restaurant is in a great location, right next to the Florence Cathedral. This central positioning makes it easily accessible and adds to the overall charm of the dining experience.

Dining Atmosphere
The atmosphere was very quiet and unhurried, creating the perfect environment for savoring each course. The pacing allowed for proper appreciation of the culinary artistry presented in each dish.
Seafood Dishes Overview
The seafood dishes were on the conservative side. The execution was solid, but the flavors leaned light and subtle, leaving me with a neutral impression. While well-prepared, they didn't particularly stand out in the overall dining experience.
Standout Dish: Spaghetti "Tireena"
The real highlight was the Spaghetti "Tireena." Made with ancient grain flour, the pasta carried a firm bite ‚Äî al dente without being undercooked, springy and full of character. The seaweed butter's briny aroma blended seamlessly with the bright citrus of bergamot, creating a clean, refreshing flavor that never felt heavy. The richness of the sea urchin paired perfectly with it, elevating the dish into something memorable.
Another Standout: The Pigeon
The pigeon was another standout. Perfectly grilled over charcoal, the meat was tender and juicy. Paired with apricot molasses and morel mushrooms, it had layers of sweetness and depth ‚Äî the kind of dish you can't stop eating, down to the last morsel clinging to the bone.
