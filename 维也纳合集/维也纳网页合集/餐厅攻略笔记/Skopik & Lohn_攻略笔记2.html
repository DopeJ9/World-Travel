Skopik & Lohn

Austrian Bistro with Recommended Dishes

This charming bistro offers excellent Austrian cuisine with a beautiful terrace at the front of the building, separated from the street by greenery. Located in the Karmeliterviertel district, it's just steps away from the Augarten Palace and its porcelain factory, museum, and park.
