Konstantin Filippou - Vienna's Michelin Two-Star Restaurant with Mediterranean-Austrian Fusion Cuisine

Konstantin Filippou

Michelin Two-Star Fine Dining with Mediterranean-Austrian Fusion

Experience Vienna's Michelin Two-Star Restaurant Konstantin Filippou, featuring exquisite Mediterranean-Austrian fusion seafood cuisine! Chef Konstantin Filippou has Greek and Austrian heritage and extensive experience working in London's Michelin-starred restaurants. The restaurant specializes in seafood that fuses Austrian and Mediterranean flavors, with a focus on black mirror surfaces and ceramic tableware, paying great attention to artistic plating and presentation.

About Konstantin Filippou Restaurant
This Michelin Two-Star restaurant specializes in seafood that fuses Austrian and Mediterranean flavors. The restaurant features a black mirror and ceramic tableware aesthetic, with great attention to artistic plating and presentation. Chef Konstantin Filippou has Greek and Austrian heritage and extensive experience working in London's Michelin-starred restaurants, perfectly blending Mediterranean flavors with Austrian traditions.
Dining Time
Selected the four-course menu, with dining time approximately 2-3 hours.
The restaurant offers different lunch and dinner menus, allowing you to choose different courses based on the time of day.
