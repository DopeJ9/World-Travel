Must-Visit Landmarks

01
Ancient Roman

Colosseum

The iconic amphitheater that hosted gladiatorial contests. A timeless symbol of Imperial Rome and one of the New Seven Wonders.
Book Tickets
02
Ancient Roman

Roman Forum

The heart of ancient political and commercial life. Walk through the ruins where emperors ruled and senators debated.
Book Tickets
03
Ancient Roman

Palatine Hill

The legendary birthplace of Rome. Home to imperial palaces with stunning panoramic views over the Forum.
Book Tickets
04
Piazza & Steps

Spanish Steps

The famous Baroque stairway connecting Piazza di Spagna with the Trinità dei Monti church. A perfect spot for "Dolce Vita".
Book Visit
05
Sacred Site

Trinity Church

Trinità dei Monti stands majestically at the top of the Spanish Steps, known for its twin bell towers and striking frescoes.
Book Visit
06
Ancient Temple

Pantheon

The best-preserved Roman building, dedicated to all gods. Famous for its massive unreinforced concrete dome and oculus.
Book Tickets
07
Culture & Arts

Rome Opera House

Teatro dell’Opera di Roma. Experience world-class opera and ballet in this historic 19th-century venue.
Book Show
08
Vatican City

Vatican Museums

Home to immense art collections gathered by Popes throughout the centuries, including the Sistine Chapel.
Book Tickets
09
Vatican City

Saint Peter's Basilica

The center of Christianity and a masterpiece of Renaissance architecture. Climb the dome for the best view of Rome.
Book Visit
