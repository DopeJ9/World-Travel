Rome Must-Visit Attractions Guide
1

Colosseum

Ancient Roman Site
The iconic amphitheater that hosted gladiatorial contests and public spectacles. A symbol of Imperial Rome and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
Book Tickets
2

Roman Forum

Ancient Roman Site
The heart of ancient Rome's political, commercial and judicial life. Walk through the ruins where emperors once ruled and senators debated.
Book Tickets
3

Palatine Hill

Ancient Roman Site
According to legend, the birthplace of Rome where Romulus and Remus were found. Home to imperial palaces with stunning views over the Forum.
Book Tickets
4

Spanish Steps

Historic Square
The famous Baroque stairway of 135 steps, built in the 18th century, connecting Piazza di Spagna with the Trinità dei Monti church.
Book Visit
5

Trinity Church

Sacred Site
A Roman Catholic church at the top of the Spanish Steps, built in the late Renaissance period with French influence, known for its striking frescoes.
Book Visit
6

Pantheon

Ancient Temple
The best-preserved Roman building, dedicated to all gods. Famous for its massive dome with an oculus opening to the sky.
Book Tickets
7

Rome Opera House

Cultural Venue
Teatro dell’Opera di Roma, originally opened in 1880 as Teatro Costanzi, today the premier opera house of Rome with world-class performances.
Book Show
8

Vatican Museums

Vatican Site
Explore one of the world's greatest art collections, including the Sistine Chapel with Michelangelo's masterpieces. Home to priceless Renaissance and ancient artifacts.
Book Tickets
9

Saint Peter's Basilica

Vatican Site
The heart of Christianity and a universal symbol of spirituality. Visit the magnificent basilica and optionally climb to Michelangelo's dome for breathtaking views.
Book Visit
