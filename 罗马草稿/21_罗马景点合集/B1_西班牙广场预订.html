Piazza di Spagna

An exquisite 18th-century square featuring the Spanish Steps and the Trinità dei Monti church on the hill, creating a perfect trio of architectural wonders.

Opening Hours

09:00 - 19:00
Monday to Sunday

Admission

FREE
No entrance fee required
Open to all visitors

What to See

The Spanish Steps (Scalinata di Trinità dei Monti)
The Spanish Steps in Rome, Italy, climb a steep slope between Piazza di Spagna at the base and Piazza Trinità dei Monti, dominated by the Trinità dei Monti church, at the top.
Trinità dei Monti
A place of prayer and welcoming in the heart of Rome.
