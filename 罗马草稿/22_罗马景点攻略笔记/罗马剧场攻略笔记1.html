Rome Opera House (Teatro Costanzi) Performance Experience

Teatro dell'Opera di Roma

Evening Opera Performance Experience

The Rome Opera House (Teatro dell'Opera di Roma), also known as Teatro Costanzi, was built in 1880 and is one of Italy's most renowned opera houses. It hosts world-class operas, ballets, and symphony performances year-round, all within an elegant, historic setting.

I attended an evening opera at Teatro Costanzi, and the whole experience felt quite ceremonial. The interior is splendid and golden, with the lighting creating a truly classic European atmosphere. Most of the audience dressed tastefullyâ€”while formal attire isn't strictly required, everyone looked very polished, so I'd recommend not being too casual.

Pro Tip: Arrive at least 30 minutes early to explore the beautiful lobby and hallways. Each floor features those iconic red velvet boxes you see in movies!
